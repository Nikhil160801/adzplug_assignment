Why Freeskout?

Freeskout is a free mobile application that allows brands and influencers/ bloggers to connect with no middlemen involved. Brands can discover Indian influencers/ bloggers on all social media platforms( Instagram, YouTube, Zomato and more) hassle-free.

How does Freeskout work?

Register your brand, set your requirements - budget, social media platform, type of influencer. Our algorithm will match you with a list of top bloggers/Influencers in India suitable for your campaign. Connect to the one that suits your brand and start chatting. All this ''Free of Cost''.
